A nonprofit movement raising awareness for people facing illness worldwide. Turn your profile picture red so the community shows up for others when they are struggling.

What is Project Red?

A simple signal that people are not alone.

01

Bring attention to people battling sickness around the world.

02

Use a red profile picture as a small, public sign of support.

How do I join?

Take the red profile picture pledge.

  1. 1

    Click the profile picture tool when it launches.

  2. 2

    Share your new red profile picture with friends and online.

  3. 3

    Invite others to join Project Red and keep the support moving.

  4. 4

    Join the Discord server for updates, news, and launch notes.

Why should I join?

Illness affects millions globally, and many people do not get the support they need.

A red profile picture is a small but powerful way to say, "I see you. I am here." Your support helps create a community that shows up for others.
